# Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

## ⚡ Overview

Before contributing to this project, you must agree to this Contributor License Agreement.

This ensures:
- Legal protection for the project
- Clear ownership of all contributions
- Consistent licensing across the codebase

A CLA defines how contributions are licensed and protects both the project and contributors. [2](https://commons-os.github.io/patterns/contributor-license-agreement-pattern/)  

---

## ✅ By contributing, you agree to:

### 1. Ownership
- You confirm that your contribution is your original work
- You have the right to submit it

### 2. License Grant
You grant the project owner:

- A **perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, irrevocable licence**
to use, modify, distribute, and relicense your contributions

### 3. Internal / Proprietary Use
- Contributions become part of a **restricted proprietary codebase**
- They follow the same LICENSE.md terms

### 4. No Attribution Requirement
- The project owner may use contributions **without attribution**

### 5. No Obligation
- Contributions may be accepted, modified, or rejected
